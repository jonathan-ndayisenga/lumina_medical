The problem we fix

Your departments have systems.
Do they talk to each other?

Most facilities run reception, lab, pharmacy and billing on separate tools or on paper. Patient data gets lost in the handoffs between them.

Before Ternah
✕Files re-registered at every department, every visit
✕Lab results walked across the compound on paper
✕Billing leaks: services rendered but never invoiced
✕Month-end reports assembled by hand, days late
With Ternah
✓One patient record shared by every department

Modular subscriptions

Patient queue management
